# The Art of Simplicity

In a world of noise, **clarity is power**. 

When building digital products, we often confuse *features* with *value*. But the true value lies in the subtraction of the unnecessary.

## Why Markdown?

Markdown is the perfect example of this philosophy. It strips away the complex formatting tools and leaves you with just the structure and the words. It's raw, it's portable, and it's beautiful.
